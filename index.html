      About

      Licious is developed for selling meat and fish food marketplace applications that help the people to buy their meal. With this application, the workers and pounders can sell their food through their agents. The app allows you to buy the fresh meat by categories. It also allows them to search the product by name. The restaurant owners can also view their past orders. The application comes with several other features to facilitate easy meal buying experience for the people. Some of these features are multiple modes of payments that include cash and cards, return policy, tracking order status, select city, product details, checkout, wishlist, and many others.

      THE CHALLENGES

      While developing this application, we came across several challenges. We had to maintain the prices and quantity of stocks. Not only this, we also had to manage all the deliveries to the location. Apart from that, we had to manage several other things like product, account details, online orders, time management and delivery partners.

      THE SOLUTION

      We understood all the challenges properly and came up with the final solution that can overcome all of it. We used the technology that made it possible to reflect any change in the price of the product that has already been added in the cart in real-time and have a portal for overview to delivery. Our solution is also feature-rich which provides a rich experience to the owners, Pounders, agents, and many more.
